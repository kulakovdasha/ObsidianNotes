## Reels structure 

The goal of this content is to inform first time home buyers or/and sellers about general information about real-estate and its transactions.

**Primary audience**: anyone who is looking to buy/home or gather general information about real-estate. 

Content is built around several formats: 
- broad education for people who aren't actively searching yet
- narrow topics targeting a specific pain point or common mistake
- personal stories from real buying and investing experience and its renovation/renting process
- plain-language breakdowns of the American real estate system for immigrants (including in Russian); 
- local market insights for NE Philly and Bucks County; and myth-busting that removes the beliefs stopping people from taking action.

The core message we want to communicate: [[Real Estate/Main Page#^6ad12b|Kiryl]] is an agent who has lived this himself. 

Each reel runs 20 - 40 seconds and speaks to one audience at a time: buyers, sellers, or investors.
1. should be between 0-30 +/- couple seconds (can be less but not much more) 
2. target sellers/buyers/investors 
3. single reel = single idea
